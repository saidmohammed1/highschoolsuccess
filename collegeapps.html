Application Fee Waivers

Don't let application fees hold you back! Learn if you're eligible for fee waivers:

You may be eligible for application fee waivers if:

  • You're enrolled in or eligible for the Federal Free or Reduced Price Lunch program
  • Your family income falls within Income Eligibility Guidelines
  • You're enrolled in a federal, state, or local program that aids students from low-income families
  • Your family receives public assistance
  • You live in federally subsidized public housing or a foster home
  • You are a ward of the state or an orphan

Speak with your school counselor to learn more and obtain fee waivers!
