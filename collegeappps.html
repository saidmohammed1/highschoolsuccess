Your College Application Journey

Application Timeline

Junior Year Spring

Research colleges, take standardized tests, plan campus visits

Summer Before Senior Year

Draft essays, prepare resume, gather recommendations

Senior Year Fall

Finalize college list, complete applications, submit early applications

Senior Year Winter

Submit regular applications, complete FAFSA, apply for scholarships

Application Checklist

  • Research and finalize college list
  • Take or retake standardized tests (SAT/ACT)
  • Request letters of recommendation
  • Write and revise personal essays
  • Complete the Common Application
  • Gather transcripts and test scores
  • Submit FAFSA and CSS Profile (if required)
  • Apply for scholarships
  • Submit applications before deadlines
  • Follow up on application status

Application Fee Waivers

Don't let application fees hold you back! You may be eligible for application fee waivers if you meet certain criteria.

You may be eligible if you:

  • Receive Free or Reduced Price Lunch
  • Are enrolled in a federal, state, or local program that aids students from low-income families
  • Your family receives public assistance
  • You live in federally subsidized public housing or a foster home
  • You are a ward of the state or an orphan

Speak with your school counselor to learn more and obtain fee waivers!
