Tips for Effective Activity Planning

  • Start early and try different activities to find your passions
  • Aim for depth over breadth - colleges value commitment and growth
  • Take on leadership roles as you progress through high school
  • Balance your activities across different areas (e.g., academics, sports, arts, community service)
  • Don't overcommit - quality is more important than quantity
  • Keep track of your accomplishments and hours spent on each activity
