Sarah's Journey to MIT

Sarah discovered her passion for robotics in her freshman year. She joined the robotics club, excelled in AP classes, and participated in national competitions. Her dedication paid off with a full scholarship to MIT.

"Finding my passion early on gave me direction and purpose throughout high school."

Marcus's Running Start Success

Marcus used Running Start to get a head start on his college credits. He balanced his time between high school sports and college classes, graduating with both his high school diploma and an Associate's degree.

"Running Start challenged me academically and prepared me for university life."

Lily's Financial Aid Victory

Lily comes from a low-income family but didn't let that stop her. She worked hard to maintain a high GPA, volunteered in her community, and successfully applied for multiple scholarships and grants.

"With determination and the right resources, college can be accessible to anyone."
